About Us

The company was founded in 2006 by two friends who met at university and shared a passion for the outdoors as well as fashion. Rainy Days was formed as a way to offer great quality rain jackets, that look good and perform well under a variety of conditions. We believe that quality shouldn't be at a sacrifice of looking good. Rainy Days is passionate about outdoor wear that'll keep you dry no matter the weather.
